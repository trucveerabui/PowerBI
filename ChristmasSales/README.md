**Christmas Sales Dashboard
This dashboard helps a retailer to analyze customer behaviors in 4 Christmas seasons (2018-2021).


Customer segment: Children segment (age 1-11) contributes nearly 40 of total Christmas sales over the years. They prefer in-store shopping and cash as a payment method. 
Covid: Sales decreased 3,2% in Covid year 2020 and increased 1,9% after that, but it had not yet reached pre-Covid levels. 
Geography: Sweden took the crown for the most Christmas sales over the 4 seasons, reaching 3,8 million Euros. The Netherlands, onces the best in season 2018/2019, faced a decline of 12% over the 4 seasons. Belgium showcased the most significant growth of 14%, climbing from the 7th to the 5th spot.
