# Coffee Sales Dashboard
Power BI dashboard analyzing coffee shop sales.

This dashboard highlights sales performance for a coffee shop chain from Jan–Jun 2023, making it easy for managers to understand trends across time, stores, and product categories.

- Total sales: $726.87K across 149K transactions.
- Store performance: Astoria and Hell’s Kitchen are neck-and-neck.
- Top categories: Coffee and Tea remain the biggest revenue drivers.
- Sales trend: A dip in April followed by a strong 77% rebound in May, and a peak in June.
- Customer habits: Peak hours 8–11 AM — right when the morning rush kicks in.
